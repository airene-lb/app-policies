Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-01-01

Overview

My Social Manager is a private social media management tool used to schedule and publish video content to connected TikTok accounts.

Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to operate the App:

  • TikTok OAuth access token and refresh token
  • TikTok open_id and display name
  • Video files you upload for publishing
  • Scheduled publishing times you set

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to authenticate with TikTok and to schedule and publish the videos you upload through the App. We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties, except as required to operate the App (e.g., sending video data to TikTok API on your behalf).

Data Retention

Access tokens are stored only as long as necessary to maintain your connected account. You may disconnect your account at any time, which will remove your stored credentials.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. Access to the App is restricted to authorized users only.

Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the App administrator.
